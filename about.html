Umrah Badal - A Sacred Act of Worship

Umrah Badal is the act of performing Umrah on behalf of someone who is unable to do so themselves. This could be due to illness, old age, or death. It is a selfless act of worship that allows Muslims to fulfill the religious obligation of Umrah for their loved ones.

01
Religious Significance
Umrah Badal is a noble deed that brings blessings to both the performer and the person on whose behalf it is performed.
02
Who Can Benefit?
Deceased loved ones, elderly individuals, and those who are ill can benefit from Umrah Badal.
03
Islamic Rulings
Supported by Islamic texts and scholarly opinions, Umrah Badal is a valid and rewarding practice.
